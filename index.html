Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Eco Vendo Machine

The Eco Vendo uses a multi-stage process: 1) Documents are securely shredded, 2) Ink is removed through our proprietary de-inking process, 3) The pulp is cleaned and refined, 4) New sheets are formed and pressed, 5) Sheets are dried and ready for use. The entire process takes about 5-7 minutes per batch.

Our machine accepts most common office papers including copy paper, printer paper, notebook paper, and even some colored papers. It cannot process cardboard, heavily laminated materials, or papers with plastic coatings. The AI sorting system will automatically reject unsuitable materials.

The output quality is comparable to standard 80-100 gsm office paper with brightness levels between 88-92 ISO. It's perfect for everyday printing, copying, and note-taking. For specialized printing needs (like high-resolution images), we recommend testing samples first.

The Eco Vendo is designed for minimal maintenance. Daily tasks include emptying the waste compartment (non-recyclable materials) and refilling the water tank (about once per week for average use). Professional servicing is recommended every 6 months or 50,000 sheets, whichever comes first.

Most businesses see ROI within 18-24 months through savings on paper purchases and reduced recycling costs. High-volume offices may achieve ROI in under a year. Our calculator can provide a customized estimate based on your current paper usage and costs.
